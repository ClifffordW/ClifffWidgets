Why watch Ambassadors?

Ambassador Giveaways: Unlike standard Twitch Drops, Ambassadors have special codes and item links to give away during their streams.
  • ✦ Klei Points & Spools
    Many ambassadors host giveaways for links that grant 1000+ Klei Points or Spools directly to your account.
  • ✦ Resurrected Bundles
    Special item skins from past events are often "resurrected" and given to ambassadors to distribute to active viewers.
  • ✦ Exclusive Twitch Drops
    Standard drops are earned by watching any Klei-enabled stream, but Ambassadors are the best place to find active drop campaigns and community interaction.
